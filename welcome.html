Enable in Incognito Mode

To block websites in incognito/private browsing mode, you need to enable the extension for incognito:

  1. Open Chrome Extensions Click the puzzle piece icon in Chrome toolbar, then click "Manage extensions"
    Or go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Find SiteBlocker Look for the red "B" icon with SiteBlocker name
  3. Click "Details" This opens the extension's settings page
  4. Enable "Allow in incognito" Toggle this switch ON to enable blocking in private windows
